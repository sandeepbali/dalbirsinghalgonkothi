Dalbir Singh
Algon Kothi

Politician & Community Leader — BJP

Born and raised in the historic border region of Khem Karan, Punjab, Dalbir Singh embodies the values, resilience, and cultural richness of the land he serves.

His journey into public service was driven by a deep-rooted desire to make a tangible difference. From organizing community gatherings and religious events to personally attending families during times of grief, his hands-on approach has set him apart.

His name, "Algon Kothi," derives from his home village — VPO Algon Kothi — a community that has been central to his identity and mission.
