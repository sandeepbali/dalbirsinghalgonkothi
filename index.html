BJP Leader • Politician & Community Leader

Dalbir Singh Algon Kothi is a dedicated politician and community leader from Khem Karan, Punjab. Representing the Bharatiya Janata Party, he works tirelessly for the welfare of Vidhan Sabha Halka KhemKaran.

With deep roots in the agricultural heartland and a strong connection to Sikh cultural traditions, he bridges traditional values and modern governance.
